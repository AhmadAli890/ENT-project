About the CE Lab Inventory System

The CE Lab Inventory System is a simple and efficient solution designed to manage and track lab equipment in real time. It helps record item details, availability, and usage history to maintain an organized and transparent inventory.

This system ensures that all assets are easily accessible and properly maintained within the lab environment. It reduces manual tracking errors and speeds up routine inventory tasks.

Project Goals

  • Provide an intuitive interface for lab staff and students.
  • Keep accurate records of instruments, tools, and materials.
  • Help with audits, maintenance planning, and allocation.
