CE Lab Inventory System

Organize and manage lab equipment effortlessly. Track items, monitor availability, and generate useful reports for audits and maintenance — designed for civil engineering labs.

Overview

The CE Lab Inventory System is a simple and efficient solution designed to manage and track lab equipment in real time. It helps record item details, availability, and usage history to maintain an organized and transparent inventory.

With a clean dashboard and reporting tools, lab administrators can reduce loss, ensure timely maintenance, and keep equipment accessible to students and staff.

Quick Features

Item Tracking

Keep detailed records for each lab item including ID, location, and condition.

Stock Monitoring

Monitor counts and receive visual cues when items are low or missing.

Reports

Generate CSV or PDF summaries for audits and lab management.

User Roles

Assign roles to staff and students to control access and actions.
